Location

There will be a map and an address on the welcome page, so just write a paragraph that explains where to find your church like you would tell a local you just met at McDonalds.

Need some help?

Don't assume people know how to get to your church. Our church has been in the same place in our small town for 50 years and we still meet people every week who don't know where it is.

The Welcome Page

This is the most important page on your website. It is a page specifically designed to turn vistors of your website into visitors of your church. The page has two goals:

  • Present a case for how your church can help the potential visitor.
  • Present information a potential visitor would need to visit your church.
